# Feature Specification: Compliance & Disclosure
**ID:** `F0005`
**Epic/Module:** Compliance & Trust
**Status:** Draft

---

## 1. Business Objective
Meet Vietnamese consumer-protection norms and Google quality guidelines for affiliate
sites by making the site's monetization and data practices transparent. This protects the
site from policy takedowns (AdSense, Shopee affiliate program), builds visitor trust, and
satisfies the "Required disclosures" rule in `CLAUDE.md`. The footer disclosure block and its
links were already delivered under F0002 (US00022); this feature closes the remaining gaps:
the two legal pages the footer points to (currently 404) and a reusable disclosure banner for
the top of every blog post.

## 2. Scope
### In Scope
* Privacy Policy page at `/chinh-sach-bao-mat/` (Vietnamese, static/SSG).
* Affiliate Disclosure page at `/cong-bo-tiep-thi-lien-ket/` (Vietnamese, static/SSG, long-form
  expansion of the footer one-liner).
* Reusable `<AffiliateDisclosure>` component rendering `AFFILIATE_DISCLOSURE_VI`, ready to be
  placed at the top of every blog post by the F0006 blog-post template.
* Both pages carry a unique `<title>`, `<meta name="description">`, and a canonical URL.
* Both pages reuse the shared `AFFILIATE_DISCLOSURE_VI` constant from `lib/disclosures.ts` (no
  duplicated copy).

### Out of Scope
* **Footer disclosure block and footer links** — already delivered under F0002 (US00022); this
  spec only fixes the broken targets those links point to.
* Cookie-consent / GA-consent banner — deferred until GA is actually enabled in production
  (per backlog F0005 out-of-scope note).
* Full JSON-LD / Open Graph / breadcrumb SEO treatment — owned by F0009. These pages get only
  the baseline title/description/canonical here.
* Wiring `<AffiliateDisclosure>` into the actual blog-post template — that placement is executed
  by F0006; US00051 only delivers the component and its placement contract.
* Legal review of the policy wording — content is written to a reasonable good-faith standard;
  professional legal sign-off is a business task outside engineering.

## 3. Process Flow
1. A visitor scrolls to the footer on any page and sees the affiliate disclosure block plus two
   links: "Chính sách bảo mật" and "Công bố tiếp thị liên kết" (already present from US00022).
2. The visitor clicks "Chính sách bảo mật" → lands on `/chinh-sach-bao-mat/` and reads the
   Vietnamese privacy policy.
3. The visitor clicks "Công bố tiếp thị liên kết" → lands on `/cong-bo-tiep-thi-lien-ket/` and
   reads the full affiliate disclosure.
4. Separately, when a visitor opens any blog post (built later in F0006), the
   `<AffiliateDisclosure>` banner appears at the top of the article, above the body content.

## 4. User Stories & Acceptance Criteria

### US00051: Reusable inline affiliate-disclosure banner
**As a** content reader,
**I want to** see the affiliate disclosure at the top of every blog post,
**So that** I know about affiliate links before reading product recommendations.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
* **Scenario 1:** Component renders the canonical text
    * `Given` the `<AffiliateDisclosure />` component
    * `When` it is rendered
    * `Then` it displays the exact `AFFILIATE_DISCLOSURE_VI` string from `lib/disclosures.ts`
      (single source of truth — no hard-coded copy in the component).

* **Scenario 2:** Single seam, reusable by F0006
    * `Given` the F0006 blog-post template will need a top-of-post disclosure
    * `When` F0006 is built
    * `Then` it can import `<AffiliateDisclosure />` and place it above the article body without
      re-implementing the text or styling.

* **Scenario 3:** Accessible, semantic, themed
    * `Given` the rendered banner
    * `When` inspected
    * `Then` it uses a semantic element appropriate for an aside/note, is distinguishable from body
      copy (uses theme tokens / `--color-primary` accent, not external fonts), and meets contrast
      requirements.

* **Scenario 4:** Server Component, co-located styles
    * `Given` project conventions
    * `When` the component is added
    * `Then` it lives at `components/AffiliateDisclosure.tsx` with co-located
      `components/AffiliateDisclosure.module.css`, is a Server Component (no `"use client"`), and
      contains no JSX-side affiliate-URL or analytics logic.

### US00052: Privacy Policy page
**As a** site visitor,
**I want to** read a Vietnamese privacy policy at `/chinh-sach-bao-mat/`,
**So that** I understand how my data (analytics, affiliate clicks) is handled and trust the site.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
* **Scenario 1:** Page renders (happy path)
    * `Given` the site is built and deployed
    * `When` I navigate to `/chinh-sach-bao-mat/`
    * `Then` a statically pre-rendered Vietnamese privacy-policy page loads with a single `<h1>`,
      semantic body content, and the global `<Header />` / `<Footer />` chrome.

* **Scenario 2:** Footer link is no longer broken
    * `Given` the footer link "Chính sách bảo mật" pointing at `/chinh-sach-bao-mat/` (from US00022)
    * `When` I click it from any page
    * `Then` I land on the privacy-policy page and receive a `200` (no `404`/`not-found`).

* **Scenario 3:** Required content present
    * `Given` I am reading the privacy-policy page
    * `When` I review its sections
    * `Then` it states: what data is collected (GA4 analytics only when enabled; no user accounts
      and no PII collected by the site), use of cookies, third-party services (Shopee affiliate,
      Google Analytics/AdSense), and a contact point.

* **Scenario 4:** Page metadata
    * `Given` the page is built
    * `When` I inspect the document head
    * `Then` it has a unique Vietnamese `<title>`, a `<meta name="description">`, and a canonical
      URL of `${NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL}/chinh-sach-bao-mat/`.

### US00053: Affiliate Disclosure page
**As a** site visitor,
**I want to** read the full affiliate-disclosure statement at `/cong-bo-tiep-thi-lien-ket/`,
**So that** I trust that the site's monetization is transparent and adds no cost to me.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
* **Scenario 1:** Page renders (happy path)
    * `Given` the site is built and deployed
    * `When` I navigate to `/cong-bo-tiep-thi-lien-ket/`
    * `Then` a statically pre-rendered Vietnamese affiliate-disclosure page loads with a single
      `<h1>`, semantic body content, and the global `<Header />` / `<Footer />` chrome.

* **Scenario 2:** Footer link is no longer broken
    * `Given` the footer link "Công bố tiếp thị liên kết" pointing at `/cong-bo-tiep-thi-lien-ket/`
      (from US00022)
    * `When` I click it from any page
    * `Then` I land on the affiliate-disclosure page and receive a `200` (no `404`/`not-found`).

* **Scenario 3:** Reuses the canonical disclosure text
    * `Given` the page body
    * `When` I read its lead paragraph
    * `Then` it renders `AFFILIATE_DISCLOSURE_VI` from `lib/disclosures.ts` verbatim (no copy-paste
      duplicate), followed by a long-form explanation of the Shopee affiliate relationship and the
      "không phát sinh thêm chi phí cho bạn" (no extra cost to you) point.

* **Scenario 4:** Page metadata
    * `Given` the page is built
    * `When` I inspect the document head
    * `Then` it has a unique Vietnamese `<title>`, a `<meta name="description">`, and a canonical

## 5. Edge Cases & Error Handling
* **Unknown sub-paths:** `/chinh-sach-bao-mat/anything` or `/cong-bo-tiep-thi-lien-ket/anything`
  should 404 normally (these are leaf static routes, not dynamic segments).
* **Disclosure text drift:** Both pages and the banner must read `AFFILIATE_DISCLOSURE_VI` from
  `lib/disclosures.ts`. If a future edit changes that constant, the footer (US00022), both legal
  pages, and the blog banner must all reflect it automatically — guard against re-introducing a
  copy-pasted literal anywhere.
* **Missing `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL`:** Canonical URL generation must follow the same env-var
  handling already used by other pages; a missing value should not crash the build (matches
  existing project behavior).
* **Empty/placeholder content:** Pages must ship with real Vietnamese copy, not lorem ipsum — an
  empty legal page would itself be a compliance failure.
* **Trailing-slash consistency:** Routes and canonical URLs use the trailing-slash form
  (`/chinh-sach-bao-mat/`) to match the footer links and the site's existing route style.

## 6. Technical Considerations
* **Stack alignment:** SSG only (App Router). Both pages are static `app/<slug>/page.tsx` Server
  Components with a `metadata` export for title/description/canonical.
* **Routes & slugs (keep Vietnamese, trailing slash):**
  * `/chinh-sach-bao-mat/` → `app/chinh-sach-bao-mat/page.tsx`
  * `/cong-bo-tiep-thi-lien-ket/` → `app/cong-bo-tiep-thi-lien-ket/page.tsx`
  These exact slugs are already hard-coded in `components/Footer.tsx` (US00022); do not rename
  them without updating the footer in the same PR.
* **Single source of truth:** All disclosure copy flows from `AFFILIATE_DISCLOSURE_VI` in
  `lib/disclosures.ts`. Per `CLAUDE.md`, that string must not be edited without updating
  `CLAUDE.md` in the same commit.
* **Component conventions:** New components are PascalCase Server Components with co-located
  `.module.css` (per the established Footer/ProductCard pattern). No `lib/` JSX.
* **SEO baseline only:** Provide unique title/description/canonical now; defer JSON-LD, Open Graph,
  breadcrumbs, and sitemap inclusion to F0009 (these legal pages should still end up in the F0009
  sitemap when that ships).
* **Dependencies:** F0002 (footer, theme tokens, layout shell). US00051 unblocks the top-of-post
  disclosure requirement that F0006 will consume.
* **Verification:** Per project rules, verify both pages in a browser (footer links resolve to
  `200`, content renders, single `<h1>`) before marking UI stories done.
