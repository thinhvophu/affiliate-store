# Feature Specification: Homepage
**ID:** `F0008`
**Epic/Module:** Core App
**Status:** Approved

---

## 1. Business Objective
Deliver the site's primary entry point at `/`. The homepage orients visitors arriving from
social or organic search, signals what the site is about in seconds, and routes them into the two
revenue-driving surfaces: the product catalog (Shopee affiliate clicks) and the editorial blog
(SEO funnel). It surfaces featured product picks (`featured: true` in product JSON), category
highlights linking into `/danh-muc/[category]/`, and a strip of the latest blog posts. The page is
pure composition — it reuses the theme/shell from F0002, the `<ProductCard>` from F0004, and the
`<PostCard>` from F0006; it adds no new data layer.

## 2. Scope
### In Scope
* A real homepage route at `app/page.tsx` (replacing the current `<h1>aff-store</h1>` stub),
  full-width (no left panel), built on F0002 theme tokens and the system font stack.
* Hero / intro section: site name, Vietnamese tagline + short orienting copy, and CTAs into
  `/san-pham/` and `/bai-viet/`.
* Featured product picks: lever off `getAllProducts()` filtered to `featured: true`, rendered with
  the existing whole-card affiliate `<ProductCard>` in a 2/3/4-col responsive grid, capped at 8,
  with a "Xem tất cả" link to `/san-pham/`.
* Category highlights: registered categories from `lib/categories.ts` rendered as links into
  `/danh-muc/[category]/`, using Vietnamese display names from `getCategoryLabels()`.
* Latest blog posts strip: the 4 newest posts from `getAllPosts()` (already sorted `publishedAt`
  descending), rendered with `<PostCard>`, plus a "Xem tất cả" link to `/bai-viet/`.
* Baseline per-page SEO metadata: unique Vietnamese `<title>`, `<meta name="description">`, and a
  canonical URL for `/` (matching the F0005/F0006 baseline precedent).

### Out of Scope
* **Full SEO treatment** — JSON-LD (`Organization`/`WebSite`), Open Graph / `og:image`, visible
  breadcrumbs, and sitemap inclusion are owned by F0009. The homepage gets only baseline
  title/description/canonical here.
* Personalization, recommendation engines, or behavioral product ranking — featured picks are
  driven solely by the `featured` flag in product JSON.
* Search, newsletter signup, promotional banners, or carousels/auto-rotating sliders.
* Any new data loader — the homepage consumes existing `lib/products.ts`, `lib/categories.ts`, and
  `lib/posts.ts` helpers as-is.
* New product card / post card variants — existing components are reused unchanged.

## 3. Process Flow
1. A visitor lands on `/` from social or organic search.
2. The hero section immediately communicates what the site is (gaming peripherals & tech gadgets,
   Vietnamese) and offers two CTAs: browse products (`/san-pham/`) or read guides (`/bai-viet/`).
3. The visitor scrolls to **Sản phẩm nổi bật** and sees up to 8 featured products as whole-card
   affiliate cards; clicking any card opens its Shopee affiliate URL in a new tab (F0003), or the
   "Xem tất cả" link takes them to the full catalog.
4. The visitor scrolls to **Danh mục** and picks a category, landing on
   `/danh-muc/[category]/`.
5. The visitor scrolls to **Bài viết mới nhất** and sees the 4 newest posts; clicking one opens the
   post, or "Xem tất cả" opens the blog listing.

## 4. User Stories & Acceptance Criteria

### US00081: Homepage shell, hero/intro section & baseline metadata
**As a** visitor arriving from social or organic search,
**I want to** immediately understand what the site offers and where to go next,
**So that** I can quickly browse products or read buying guides.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
* **Scenario 1:** Real homepage replaces the stub (Happy Path)
    * `Given` the project currently renders `<h1>aff-store</h1>` at `/`
    * `When` I open `/`
    * `Then` I see a full-width homepage (no left panel) styled with F0002 theme tokens and the
      system font stack, containing a hero section with the site name, a Vietnamese tagline, short
      orienting copy, and two CTAs linking to `/san-pham/` and `/bai-viet/`
    * `And` the page renders a single `<h1>` and uses semantic landmarks (the existing
      `<main>` from the root layout).

* **Scenario 2:** Baseline SEO metadata
    * `Given` the homepage route exists
    * `When` the page is built (SSG)
    * `Then` it exports `metadata` with a unique Vietnamese `<title>`, a `<meta name="description">`,
      and a canonical URL resolving to `/`
    * `And` the canonical base respects `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL`.

* **Scenario 3:** CTA navigation
    * `Given` I am on the homepage
    * `When` I click the "Sản phẩm" CTA (then, separately, the "Bài viết" CTA)
    * `Then` I navigate to `/san-pham/` (and `/bai-viet/` respectively).

### US00082: Featured product picks section
**As a** visitor on the homepage,
**I want to** see a curated set of highlighted products,
**So that** I can discover popular picks and click through to buy.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
* **Scenario 1:** Featured grid renders (Happy Path)
    * `Given` one or more products have `featured: true` in their JSON
    * `When` I view the homepage
    * `Then` a "Sản phẩm nổi bật" section renders up to 8 featured products using the existing
      whole-card affiliate `<ProductCard>` in a responsive grid (2 cols mobile / 3 tablet / 4 desktop)
    * `And` each card opens its Shopee affiliate URL in a new tab with the F0003 link semantics.

* **Scenario 2:** "Xem tất cả" link
    * `Given` the featured section is displayed
    * `When` I click "Xem tất cả"
    * `Then` I navigate to `/san-pham/`.

* **Scenario 3:** Cap enforced
    * `Given` more than 8 products are flagged `featured: true`
    * `When` I view the section
    * `Then` exactly 8 cards are shown (deterministic order — by the existing product load order).

* **Scenario 4:** Empty state
    * `Given` no product has `featured: true`
    * `When` I view the homepage
    * `Then` the featured section either renders a Vietnamese empty message or is omitted entirely,
      and the rest of the homepage still renders without error.

### US00083: Category highlights section
**As a** visitor on the homepage,
**I want to** see the product categories at a glance,
**So that** I can jump straight into the category I care about.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
* **Scenario 1:** Category links render (Happy Path)
    * `Given` categories are registered in `lib/categories.ts`
    * `When` I view the homepage
    * `Then` a "Danh mục" section renders one link per registered category, each labelled with its
      Vietnamese display name from `getCategoryLabels()`.

* **Scenario 2:** Navigation
    * `Given` the category highlights are displayed
    * `When` I click a category
    * `Then` I navigate to `/danh-muc/[category]/` for that category's slug.

* **Scenario 3:** Source of truth
    * `Given` a new category is added to `lib/categories.ts`
    * `When` the site is rebuilt
    * `Then` the new category automatically appears in the homepage highlights (no hard-coded list).

### US00084: Latest blog posts strip
**As a** visitor on the homepage,
**I want to** see the newest blog posts,
**So that** I can read fresh buying guides and reviews.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
* **Scenario 1:** Latest posts render (Happy Path)
    * `Given` published posts exist
    * `When` I view the homepage
    * `Then` a "Bài viết mới nhất" section renders the 4 newest posts (by `publishedAt` descending,
      via `getAllPosts()`) using the existing `<PostCard>`.

* **Scenario 2:** "Xem tất cả" link
    * `Given` the latest-posts strip is displayed
    * `When` I click "Xem tất cả"
    * `Then` I navigate to `/bai-viet/`.

* **Scenario 3:** Fewer than 4 posts
    * `Given` only N posts exist where 0 < N < 4
    * `When` I view the section
    * `Then` exactly N post cards are shown without layout breakage.

* **Scenario 4:** No posts
    * `Given` no posts exist
    * `When` I view the homepage
    * `Then` the latest-posts section is omitted (not an empty shell) and the page renders without error.

## 5. Edge Cases & Error Handling
* **No featured products:** featured section degrades gracefully (Vietnamese empty message or full
  omission) — see US00082 Scenario 4.
* **No posts:** latest-posts section is omitted — see US00084 Scenario 4.
* **Missing product image:** `<ProductCard>` already enforces `images.length >= 1` at build time
  (US00046/products loader); the homepage inherits that guarantee and renders images via `next/image`.
* **Long Vietnamese titles:** product/post card title clamping is owned by `<ProductCard>` /
  `<PostCard>`; the homepage must not re-implement clamping.
* **`NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` unset in dev:** canonical falls back to the relative `/` path; build-time
  metadata must not throw.
* **Affiliate links:** every product card click must route through `<AffiliateLink>` (no raw
  Shopee anchors) — enforced by reusing `<ProductCard>`.

## 6. Technical Considerations
* **SSG only.** The homepage is a Server Component pre-rendered at build time; all data
  (`getAllProducts()`, `CATEGORIES`/`getCategoryLabels()`, `getAllPosts()`) is read at build time.
  No client-side data fetching, no `"use client"` on the page itself.
* **Composition, not new infra.** Reuse `<ProductCard>` (F0004), `<PostCard>` (F0006), theme tokens
  and layout primitives (F0002). Each homepage section may be its own Server Component under
  `components/` with a co-located `<Name>.module.css` (project convention).
* **No left panel.** The homepage is full-width; it does not use `<ShellLayout>`.
* **Single source of truth for taxonomy/prices/dates.** Category labels come from
  `lib/categories.ts`; prices render via `formatVnd()`; dates via `formatPostDate()` — never
  hand-rolled on the homepage.
* **Affiliate compliance.** All product CTAs route through `<AffiliateLink>` via `<ProductCard>`;
  no raw affiliate anchors on the homepage (F0003/US00034).
* **SEO baseline only.** Provide title/description/canonical via the route `metadata` export.
  JSON-LD, Open Graph, breadcrumbs, and sitemap inclusion are deferred to F0009 and should pick the
  homepage up when they ship.
* **Caps are explicit constants.** Featured cap (8) and latest-posts count (4) live as named
  constants in the relevant section component, not magic numbers scattered in JSX.
* **Verify in browser** at mobile (≤768px), tablet (768–1280px), and desktop (≥1280px) breakpoints
  before marking any story done (project UI rule).
